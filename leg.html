Workout Routine 2

Jumping Jacks

Instructions for Jumping Jacks:

  1. Stand with your feet together and your arms by your sides.
  2. In one motion, jump your feet out to the sides and raise your arms above your head.
  3. Jump back to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Squats

Instructions for Squats:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and toes slightly turned out.
  2. Bend your knees and lower your hips as if sitting back into a chair.
  3. Keep your chest up and core engaged.
  4. Push through your heels to stand back up.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Side Lying Leg Lift

Instructions for Side Lying Leg Lift:

  1. Lie on your side with your legs extended and stacked on top of each other.
  2. Rest your head on your arm or use a pillow for support.
  3. Engage your core and lift the top leg towards the ceiling.
  4. Lower the leg back down with control.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise and switch sides.

Backward Lunge

Instructions for Backward Lunge:

  1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart and your hands on your hips.
  2. Take a step backward with your right foot.
  3. Bend both knees to lower your body into a lunge position.
  4. Push through your front heel to return to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise and switch legs.

Donkey Kicks

Instructions for Donkey Kicks:

  1. Start on all fours with your hands directly under your shoulders and knees under your hips.
  2. Engage your core and lift one leg, keeping it bent at a 90-degree angle.
  3. Extend the leg back and up, squeezing your glutes.
  4. Lower the leg back down with control.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise and switch legs.

Knee-to-Chest Stretch

Instructions for Knee-to-Chest Stretch:

  1. Lie on your back with your legs extended.
  2. Bend one knee and hug it towards your chest.
  3. Hold the stretch for a few seconds, feeling the gentle stretch in your lower back and hip.
  4. Switch legs and repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Left Quad Stretch

Instructions for Left Quad Stretch:

  1. Stand tall with your feet hip-width apart.
  2. Bend your left knee and grab your left foot or ankle with your left hand.
  3. Pull your left heel towards your glutes, feeling a stretch in the front of your left thigh.
  4. Hold the stretch for a few seconds and release.
  5. Switch legs and repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Side Hop

Instructions for Side Hop:

  1. Stand with your feet together and your arms by your sides.
  2. Jump to the right, extending your right leg out to the side and swinging your left arm across your body.
  3. Land softly on your right foot and bring your left foot back to the starting position.
  4. Repeat on the left side.
  5. Continue alternating sides for the duration of the exercise.

Sumo Squat Calf Raise

Instructions for Sumo Squat Calf Raise:

  1. Stand with your feet wider than shoulder-width apart and toes turned outwards.
  2. Lower your body into a sumo squat position by bending your knees and pushing your hips back.
  3. Rise up onto the balls of your feet, lifting your heels off the ground.
  4. Lower your heels back down to the ground.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Calf Stretch

Instructions for Calf Stretch:

  1. Stand facing a wall or sturdy object.
  2. Place your hands on the wall at shoulder height.
  3. Step back with one foot and keep it straight, while bending the front knee.
  4. Press your back heel down towards the ground, feeling a stretch in your calf muscle.
  5. Hold the stretch for the duration of the exercise.
  6. Repeat on the other leg.
