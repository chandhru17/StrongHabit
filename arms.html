Workout Routine

Jumping Jacks

Instructions for Jumping Jacks:

  1. Stand with your feet together and your arms by your sides.
  2. In one motion, jump your feet out to the sides and raise your arms above your head.
  3. Jump back to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Chest Press Pulse

Instructions for Chest Press Pulse:

  1. Lie flat on your back on a bench or mat with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
  2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand and extend your arms straight up over your chest.
  3. Lower the dumbbells towards your chest, keeping your elbows slightly bent.
  4. Push the dumbbells back up to the starting position, squeezing your chest muscles at the top.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Leg Barbell Curl

Instructions for Leg Barbell Curl:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell behind your legs.
  2. Keeping your upper body stationary, curl the barbell up towards your buttocks by contracting your hamstrings.
  3. Lower the barbell back down to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Diagonal Plank

Instructions for Diagonal Plank:

  1. Start in a plank position with your forearms on the ground and your body in a straight line.
  2. Rotate your hips to the right and lift your right arm off the ground, extending it towards the ceiling.
  3. Hold for a moment and then return to the starting position.
  4. Repeat on the left side.
  5. Alternate sides for the duration of the exercise.

Wall Pushups

Instructions for Wall Pushups:

  1. Stand facing a wall with your arms extended and your hands placed on the wall at shoulder height.
  2. Step back slightly and position your feet shoulder-width apart.
  3. Bend your elbows and lean your body towards the wall, keeping your back straight.
  4. Push back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Inchworms

Instructions for Inchworms:

  1. Stand tall with your feet hip-width apart.
  2. Bend forward at the waist and place your hands on the floor in front of you.
  3. Walk your hands forward until you are in a plank position.
  4. Take small steps with your feet to walk your hands back towards your feet.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.
