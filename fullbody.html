Workout Routine

Jumping Jacks

Instructions for Jumping Jacks:

  1. Stand with your feet together and your arms by your sides.
  2. In one motion, jump your feet out to the sides and raise your arms above your head.
  3. Jump back to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Incline Pushups

Instructions for Incline Pushups:

  1. Find an elevated surface, such as a bench or step.
  2. Place your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart on the surface.
  3. Step your feet back into a plank position with your body forming a straight line.
  4. Bend your elbows and lower your chest towards the surface.
  5. Push back up to the starting position.
  6. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Knee Pushups

Instructions for Knee Pushups:

  1. Start on your hands and knees, with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your upper body towards the floor by bending your elbows.
  3. Keep your back straight and your knees on the ground.
  4. Push back up to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Pushups

Instructions for Pushups:

  1. Start in a plank position with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your body towards the floor by bending your elbows.
  3. Keep your body straight and your core engaged.
  4. Push back up to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Wide-Arm Pushups

Instructions for Wide-Arm Pushups:

  1. Start in a plank position with your hands positioned wider than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your body towards the floor by bending your elbows.
  3. Keep your body straight and your core engaged.
  4. Push back up to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Cobra Stretch

Instructions for Cobra Stretch:

  1. Lie flat on your stomach with your hands positioned next to your shoulders.
  2. Press your hands into the floor and lift your chest off the ground.
  3. Keep your hips and legs relaxed.
  4. Hold the stretch for the duration of the exercise.

Chest Stretch

Instructions for Chest Stretch:

  1. Stand tall with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Interlace your fingers behind your back with your palms facing inwards.
  3. Squeeze your shoulder blades together and gently lift your arms.
  4. Hold the stretch for the duration of the exercise.
