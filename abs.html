Workout Routine 3

Jumping Jacks

Instructions for Jumping Jacks:

  1. Stand with your feet together and your arms by your sides.
  2. In one motion, jump your feet out to the sides and raise your arms above your head.
  3. Jump back to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Abdominal Crunches

Instructions for Abdominal Crunches:

  1. Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
  2. Place your hands behind your head or across your chest.
  3. Engage your core and lift your upper body off the floor, bringing your chest towards your knees.
  4. Lower your upper body back down to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Russian Twist

Instructions for Russian Twist:

  1. Sit on the floor with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground.
  2. Lean back slightly, keeping your back straight and core engaged.
  3. Clasp your hands together in front of your chest or hold a weight.
  4. Twist your torso to the right, bringing your clasped hands or weight towards the right side of your body.
  5. Return to the center and then twist to the left side.
  6. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Mountain Climber

Instructions for Mountain Climber:

  1. Start in a push-up position with your hands directly under your shoulders and your body in a straight line.
  2. Engage your core and bring one knee towards your chest, then quickly switch and bring the other knee forward.
  3. Continue alternating the movement of your knees, as if you are running in place.
  4. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Heel Touch

Instructions for Heel Touch:

  1. Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground.
  2. Extend your arms by your sides.
  3. Crunch up and reach with your right hand towards your right heel.
  4. Return to the starting position and repeat on the left side.
  5. Continue alternating the movement, touching your heels with each hand.
  6. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Leg Raises

Instructions for Leg Raises:

  1. Lie on your back with your legs extended and your arms by your sides.
  2. Engage your core and lift both legs off the ground, keeping them straight.
  3. Lower your legs back down with control, without touching the ground.
  4. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Plank

Instructions for Plank:

  1. Start in a push-up position with your hands directly under your shoulders and your body in a straight line.
  2. Lower yourself onto your forearms, keeping your elbows directly under your shoulders.
  3. Engage your core and hold the position, keeping your body straight and avoiding any sagging or lifting of the hips.
  4. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Spine Lumbar Twist

Instructions for Spine Lumbar Twist:

  1. Sit on the floor with your legs extended in front of you.
  2. Bend your right knee and cross your right foot over your left leg, placing it on the floor.
  3. Place your left hand on your right knee and gently twist your torso to the right, looking over your right shoulder.
  4. Hold the stretch for a few seconds, feeling the twist in your spine.
  5. Switch sides and repeat for the duration of the exercise.

Cobra Stretch

Instructions for Cobra Stretch:

  1. Lie on your stomach with your legs extended and the tops of your feet flat on the ground.
  2. Place your hands directly under your shoulders, fingers pointing forward.
  3. Press through your hands and lift your chest off the ground, keeping your hips and legs relaxed.
  4. Hold the stretch for a few seconds, feeling the gentle stretch in your abdomen and lower back.
  5. Repeat for the duration of the exercise.
